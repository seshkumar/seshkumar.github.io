Sravani and Sesh

On suggestion of several coffee experts, few months ago, we began our experiments with the flavors

  • Sravani
  • Sesh

    • They had an interesting connection towards perfect blend as they complement and complete each other. Experts decided on a ¨big day¨ to declare the blend official through a series of rituals, which are considered auspicious- Kanyadanam (the giving away of daughter by the father), Panigrahana (the groom takes the bride's hand as a sign of their union) and Saptapadi(seven vows).

    Your company would give us immense pleasure on this auspicious occassion.
